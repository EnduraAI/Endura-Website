About Endura

Independent advice, informed by execution.

Endura combines senior offshore judgement with disciplined front-end engineering. The consultancy stays focused on the decisions that establish scope, cost basis, commercial strategy and the path to execution.

The consultancy

Specialist support for late life and decommissioning decisions.

Endura Decommissioning is the consultancy offering of Van Iersel Projects Pty Ltd. It supports operators and delivery teams where inherited scope, uncertain data or unresolved interfaces are preventing a clear decision.

The role is independent and vendor-neutral. decomm.ai is introduced as a separate platform offering only where documentation intelligence would materially improve the basis.

Jed van Iersel

Jed has more than 20 years of offshore decommissioning, heavy-lift and demolition experience. Endura was established to bring execution-grounded judgement into the planning stage, where scope, cost and risk decisions are still changeable.

Where decomm.ai fits

Asset intelligence for decisions constrained by inherited data.

decomm.ai structures source records, scores documentation integrity and helps teams identify which gaps are most likely to affect scope confidence and targeted cost drivers.

The platform is commercially separated from the consultancy offer. It can support an Endura engagement or be considered independently.

Boundary kept explicitdecomm.ai informs the engineering and estimate basis. It does not set the accounting provision or replace engineering review.

Who the work supports

Built for teams carrying liability, assurance or delivery obligations.

The same asset can look different to a board, an engineering team, a contractor and a regulator. Endura structures the work so each group can see the basis, the limits and the decision path.

EPC and specialist contractors
