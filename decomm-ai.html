04

Decision support, not a black box

Trust

How the number is built, human in the loop

  • Automated extraction and scoring is the starting point, not the answer
  • Senior engineers verify every output against source documents
  • Ranges follow AACE estimate classes with documented assumptions
  • Final engineering, cost and regulatory positions are signed off by the technical team

What decomm.ai does not do

  • Replace engineering judgement or regulatory approval
  • Certify structural, lifting or marine operations
  • Guarantee a cost outcome or remove project-specific verification
  • Underwrite execution, it informs provisioning, it does not bear the liability
