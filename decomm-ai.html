decomm.ai

Make uncertainty in inherited data visible.

decomm.ai is a separate asset-intelligence platform offering for late-life and decommissioning decisions constrained by inherited data.

Platform position

From source records to decision intelligence.

The platform structures source documentation, scores integrity by domain, identifies variance drivers and provides a controlled view of regulatory, environmental and operational influences.

It informs the engineering and ARO provision basis. It does not produce the accounting number or replace engineering judgement.

Public demonstration boundaryAll interface examples are sanitised. Competitive rate structures, domain weightings and proprietary model logic are not exposed.

Four-layer architecture

A controlled route from source documents to decision surfaces.

The public architecture shows the workflow without exposing the proprietary rate structure, weighting logic or internal model configuration.

1 · Source documentation

Drawings, registers, inspection records, hazardous-material data, well information and other asset evidence.

2 · Structuring and normalisation

Records aligned to asset, discipline, revision, domain and traceability requirements.

3 · Modules A to F

Integrity, sensitivity, regulatory, environmental, readiness and closure intelligence.

4 · Decision surfaces

Command Centre, regulatory closure and scenario views used to support action.
