Indicative self-check

Eight evidence questions. One clear starting position.

This tool runs entirely in your browser. Answers are not transmitted or stored. The result is a first-pass indicator only and is not an engineering assessment, estimate classification or regulatory opinion.

How to answerChoose the position that can be demonstrated from current source records, not the position the team expects to be true.

Question 4 of 8Is the hazardous-material and NORM inventory current, classified and traceable?
