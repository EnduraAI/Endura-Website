How we deliver

Discover → support

A single, structured pathway from first data to delivered outcome, one team, one auditable trail, no hand-offs between disconnected firms.

01 · DISCOVER

Gather

All available data, drawings, surveys, cost history and regulatory correspondence.

02 · DIAGNOSE

Test

Data quality assessed; locate where gaps move cost, schedule or compliance.

03 · DEFINE

Set the basis

Scope, options, regulatory pathway and waste strategy defined.

04 · MODEL

Run scenarios

decomm.ai tests cost, schedule, waste and regulatory outcomes.

05 · PLAN

Convert

Into FEED, estimate, schedule and tender-ready material.

06 · SUPPORT

Stand alongside

Advisory support through contractor engagement and execution, assurance, not sign-off.
