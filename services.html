Services

Technical support around the decision that matters.

Endura’s service structure is concentrated on decommissioning studies, ARO basis development, business cases, scope definition, technical assurance and execution strategy.

01

Comparative assessment and business case

Options assessed against a consistent safety, environmental, technical, regulatory, schedule and cost basis.

  • Decision criteria and option framing
  • Comparative assessment or BPEO-style support where applicable
  • Business-case narrative and recommendation
  • Assumptions, exclusions and evidence position
02

Decommissioning plan development

Programmes and close-out pathways structured around the jurisdiction, decision gate and supporting evidence.

  • Scope and boundary definition
  • Programme structure and planning basis
  • Stakeholder and evidence requirements
  • Close-out and relinquishment pathway support
03

ARO basis, cost and schedule support

Engineering support behind the provision basis, with scope logic and cost-driver limitations stated clearly.

  • Basis-of-estimate support
  • Quantity and assumption review
  • Schedule logic and key dependencies
  • Targeted estimate-class language only where supported
04

Method and engineering development

Removal, isolation, demolition, logistics and well P&A methodology support developed to the required study stage.

  • Removal and preparation concepts
  • Constructability and temporary-state considerations
  • Transport, load-in and disposal interface review
  • Execution method development support
05

Contracting and execution strategy

Packaging, interface and commercial strategy that makes accountabilities visible before tendering.

  • Scope packaging
  • Contract model considerations
  • Interface and demarcation registers
  • Tender strategy and technical response support
06

Regulatory and permitting pathway

Pathway mapping and evidence planning aligned to the applicable jurisdiction and the actual asset scope.

  • Regulatory obligation mapping
  • Submission support
  • Evidence and status tracking
  • Close-out and permit-relief planning
07

Owner’s engineer and delivery support

Independent technical oversight where continuity from planning into delivery adds value.

  • Technical assurance and method review
  • Interface and scope management
  • Client-representative support
  • Defined small-scope delivery support where agreed
08

Documentation gap review

A structured view of what the inherited record set supports and what should be remediated first.

  • Source-register review
  • Critical gap identification
  • Decision-impact framing
  • Optional decomm.ai pathway
Typical outputs

What the client receives.

Outputs are tailored to the decision stage. The objective is a controlled basis that can move into review, procurement, remediation or execution without avoidable rework.

Study report

Decision statement, basis, options, findings, limitations and recommended next actions.

Technical basis

Scope boundaries, assumptions, interfaces, methodology and evidence position.

Commercial support

Cost and schedule basis, work packaging and tender or contracting inputs.

Action plan

Prioritised next steps, ownership, dependencies and information-remediation needs.
