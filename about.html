About Endura

Two decades offshore. One mission onshore and off: close the gap before it costs you.

Endura Decommissioning is an Australian specialist in offshore decommissioning and late-life asset management, supporting Tier 1 contractors and operators across Asia–Pacific. We exist to close the gap that quietly drives most decommissioning overruns: the distance between what the documentation says an asset is, and what it actually takes to safely retire it.

01

Why Endura exists

The story

Decommissioning rarely fails in execution first. It fails in planning, in the assumptions, the missing as-builts, the cost bases nobody stress-tested, and the bill arrives years later, offshore, when it is most expensive to be wrong.

After two decades delivering offshore removal, heavy-lift and late-life scopes, Jed van Iersel kept seeing the same pattern: operators carrying enormous retirement liabilities built on documentation no one on the current team could fully defend. The engineering wasn't the hard part. The quality of the information feeding the engineering was.

He founded Endura in 2025 to fix that at the planning stage, pairing senior, hands-on decommissioning engineering with a data model that measures documentation integrity and turns it into a defensible, costed position. Smarter, safer, more sustainable outcomes start with an honest view of what you actually know about the asset.

The vision, decomm.ai

Where this goes

Endura's long-term vision is a decommissioning cost-intelligence model that doesn't just describe an asset's documentation, it predicts the cost of retiring it, accurately, from the variables that actually move the number.

→

Regulation-aware

Cost and pathway change with jurisdiction, OSPAR, NOPSEMA, BSEE. decomm.ai is built to reason about the regime the asset sits under, not a generic average.

→

Asset-specific

Structure type, age, condition, water depth, materials, hazardous inventory, the model weights what genuinely drives severance, lift and disposal cost.

→

Evidence-led

It learns from documentation integrity and real project data, so its predictions get sharper as the evidence base grows, always verified by senior engineers.

The goal is simple to state and hard to do well: give an operator, a board or a JV partner a cost they can defend, before they commit capital, and long before the vessels mobilise. The platform is decision support; final engineering, cost and regulatory positions always rest with the client and their appointed authorities.
